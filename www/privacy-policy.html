Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Welcome to MindSpark ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our analytical thinking puzzle game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

MindSpark does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and version
  • Usage Data: Game statistics, puzzle completion rates, time spent, and feature usage
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data about how you interact with the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game functionality
  • To improve and optimize the user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and app performance
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To display relevant advertisements (see Section 5)
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

Your game progress, preferences, and statistics are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage or device storage. This data includes:

  • Current streak count and history
  • Puzzles completed
  • Badges and achievements earned
  • Game preferences (sound, haptics)
  • Session statistics

3.2 Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you:

  • Clear your browser cache/data
  • Uninstall the app
  • Use the "Reset Progress" feature in settings

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google AdMob (Mobile App)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our mobile app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Location data (approximate)
  • Ad interaction data

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google AdSense (Web Version)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our web version. AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to show personalized ads.

Learn more: How Google uses data

4.3 Google Analytics (Optional)

We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This service collects anonymized usage data.

Learn more: Google Analytics Privacy

5. Advertising

5.1 How Ads Work

MindSpark displays advertisements to support free access to the game. You may:

  • Watch rewarded video ads to earn in-game streak points
  • See banner or interstitial ads during gameplay

5.2 Personalized Advertising

Our advertising partners may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Web: Visit Google Ad Settings

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MindSpark is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the email below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided below.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored locally on your device (not on our servers)
  • HTTPS encryption for web communications
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited data collection (only what's necessary)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Controls

9.1 Data Management

You can manage your data at any time:

  • Reset Progress: Delete all game data via Settings → Reset Progress
  • Disable Sounds: Turn off sound effects in Settings
  • Disable Haptics: Turn off vibration feedback in Settings

9.2 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt out of analytics tracking by:

  • Using browser privacy modes (Incognito/Private)
  • Installing browser extensions that block analytics
  • Disabling JavaScript (may affect app functionality)

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using MindSpark, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of MindSpark after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Links

MindSpark may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: MindSpark does not sell your personal information.

14. Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to Do Not Track signals. MindSpark does not respond to Do Not Track signals at this time.

  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws
