Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MindSpark ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

1. Information Collection

MindSpark is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., via Feedback form).

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect non-personal information about your device, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage data (e.g., time spent, puzzles solved) via analytics
  • Crash reports to help us improve stability

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality (e.g., saving your progress locally)
  • Improve the user experience and fix bugs
  • Display non-personalized advertisements via Google AdMob

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

4. Data Storage

Your game progress (streaks, badges, scores) is stored locally on your device. Clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App may result in the loss of this progress.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

6. Children's Privacy

MindSpark is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
