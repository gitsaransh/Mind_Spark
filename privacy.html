Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

Welcome to MindSpark! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

MindSpark is designed to be privacy-first. We collect minimal data necessary for the app's functionality and monetization.

  • Game Progress: Your streaks, puzzle solutions, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Ad Data: We use Google AdMob and AdSense to show advertisements. These services may collect device identifiers, approximate location, and interaction data to show relevant ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To maintain your game progress (streaks, unlocked levels).
  • To display rewarded video ads that allow you to earn in-game hints.
  • To analyze aggregate, anonymous usage trends (e.g., "Level 5 is too hard") to improve the game.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google AdMob / AdSense: For advertising. Read Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

MindSpark is a general audience app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Our ad settings are configured to be compliant with family policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: support@mindsparkapp.com
